FAQ

  • Faq

How often should I schedule a powerwashing service?

We recommend scheduling a powerwashing service once every 1-2 years to maintain the cleanliness and appearance of your property.

What areas can you powerwash?

We can powerwash a variety of surfaces, including siding, driveways, decks, patios, fences, and more. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Is powerwashing safe for all surfaces?

Powerwashing is generally safe for most surfaces when done correctly by professionals. However, certain delicate materials may require alternative cleaning methods. We assess each surface and use appropriate techniques to ensure safety and effectiveness.

How long does a powerwashing service typically take?

The duration of a powerwashing service depends on various factors, such as the size of the area, the level of dirt or grime, and the complexity of the project. We can provide you with an estimate after assessing your specific requirements.

Do I need to be present during the powerwashing service?

It is not necessary for you to be present during the powerwashing service. However, we recommend providing access to the areas that need to be cleaned and ensuring clear communication regarding any specific requirements or concerns.

What preparation is needed before a powerwashing service?

Prior to the powerwashing service, please ensure that all windows and doors are closed securely. Move any fragile or valuable items away from the cleaning area to prevent accidental damage. We will take care of the rest and ensure a thorough cleaning process.

Do you use environmentally friendly cleaning products?

Yes, we prioritize the use of environmentally friendly cleaning products that are safe for your property and the surrounding environment. We strive to minimize our ecological footprint while delivering exceptional results.

Can powerwashing remove all stains?

Powerwashing is highly effective in removing various stains, including dirt, mildew, algae, and grime. However, the success of stain removal depends on the specific type of stain and the surface it has affected. We assess each case individually to determine the best approach.

Is powerwashing covered by insurance?

Yes, we have liability insurance coverage to protect our customers and our team. In the unlikely event of any damage occurring during the powerwashing process, we will work with you to address the situation and resolve any issues.

How can I schedule a powerwashing service?

To schedule a powerwashing service, simply contact our team through our website or give us a call. We will be happy to discuss your requirements, provide an estimate, and find a convenient time to perform the service.
